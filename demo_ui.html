You: Should I migrate my monolithic application to microservices?
Council Response:
Strategist 85% confidence

Consider your long-term scalability needs. Microservices provide better scaling but require more operational complexity.

Pragmatist 72% confidence

Start with extracting one service first. Full migration carries significant risk and cost.

Guardian 90% confidence

Ensure proper security boundaries between services. Each microservice increases your attack surface.

Economist 78% confidence

Migration costs will be significant. Budget for 2-3x initial estimates and consider ROI timeline.

Council Consensus:

Gradual Migration Recommended - Start with a pilot service to validate approach. The council agrees (78% consensus) that immediate full migration carries unnecessary risk.
